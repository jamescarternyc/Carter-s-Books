Jacob Carter

Author, Father, Entrepreneur

Jacob. L Carter was born and raised in Mount Vernon which sits just outside of the Bronx, NY. After serving in the United States Airforce for 4 years, Jacob was honorably discharged. Shortly afterwards, Jacob began work as a communications engineer and relocated to District Heights, MD, where has lived for the past 5 years.

Jacob is the father of 3 beautiful children, a son and 2 daughters. Through life experiences and a search for consciousness he became unsatisfied with society's overall lack of resources regarding black culture and black youth. Inspired by fatherhood and anxious to deliver a solution, Jacob decided to write childrens books.

Who Am I ? is Jacob's first venture into the world of self-publishing and inspiring others. However, it will not be his last. He is currently working on another book dedicated to getting a positive message out to black youth that the world can benefit from.
