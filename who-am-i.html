WHO AM I?

Jacob has grown confused by the information in school books and taught by teachers. He notices that throughout the pages of history he very rarely sees people that look like him. From this confusion, a question enters Jacob's mind. One which he will ask to the adults in his life and will take him on a journey of awakening.
